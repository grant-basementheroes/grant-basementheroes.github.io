About

I'm a passionate, industry-experienced game programmer and designer who has worked on dozens of titles across multiple platforms, console generations, and genres. My objective hasn't changed since I got into the industry 22 years ago: make good games, and get better at it every year.

I lead engineering teams as both an individual contributor and a mentor, with deep experience in production architecture, live ops, and the systems that hold a game together — monetization, analytics, UI, and tooling. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics with a focus on Computer Science from the University of Waterloo.
