Max Payne 3

Rockstar Games · Game Designer / Lead Scripter · 2007–2012 · Xbox 360, PS3, PC

Designed and implemented several levels of Max Payne 3 from paper design through prototyping, production, and final shipping. Working in Rockstar's proprietary scripting language and animation tools, I brought the gameplay and vision of each mission to screen — AI, gameplay difficulty, cutscenes, custom animations, audio, music, and level progression all had a significant scripted component.

  • Managed a team of 5 scripters for 2 years while maintaining my own scripting and design work.
  • Coordinated with scripting teams across 4 international studios.
  • Built initial level layouts (grey boxes) in 3D Studio Max.
